Amritsar Srinagar Jammu Tour Package - 9 Nights / 10 Days

Duration: 9 Nights / 10 Days


Day 01 : Amritsar :Pickup from Amritsar Railway Station/ Airport transfer to the hotel, Check-in the Hotel, Start your city tour with the blessings of Golden Temple- the most sacred temple for Sikhs. It is a symbol of the magnificence and strength of the Sikh people all over the world. Then visit Jallianwala Bagh which is a memorial to the countless numbers of innocent Indians which were massacred by General Dyer in April 13, 1919. British General, Edward Dyer, ordered his soldiers to opened fire on an unarmed gathering of Hundreds men, women and children in this place.


In the evening visit Indo-Pak Wagah Border which is famous for its ‘Retreat’ ceremony at the time of sunset. Wagah Border is situated at a distance of 28 kilometers from Amritsar. This place to see the change of guards ceremony and the flag hoisting and lowering activities which are done with great skill and precision by the border security forces of both the countries, Wagah is the only land border open between Pakistan and India, Return to hotel. Stay overnight at Hotel in Amritsar.


DAY 02 : Amritsar : Katra (275Kms): After having the breakfast at the hotel, drive to Katra via Pathankot On arrival at Katra, check into your hotel. You may leave for darshan in the evening (or next morning, as per your choice).Stay overnight at Hotel in Katra.


DAY 03 : Katra: After having the breakfast at the hotel, Start trekking to holy town of Katra is the starting point of the 14 km (approx.) trek to the holy shrine of Mata Vaishno Devi Jee, After the Darshan return back to the Hotel in the evening, Stay overnight at Hotel in Katra


Day 04 : Katra-Patnitop (80 Kms) :After having the breakfast at the hotel, leave for the beautiful hills of Patnitop, at an altitude of 2024 m, across which the Jammu-Srinagar highway passes. Patnitop offers beautiful picnic spots, peaceful walks and breathtaking views of the valley of the Chenab basin. Check in the hotel, After relaxing go for local sightseeing which includes Nag Temple and excursion to Nathatop etc. Day at leisure to enjoy the natural beauty. Stay overnight at Hotel in Patnitop.


Day 05 : Patnitop-Srinagar: After having the breakfast at the hotel, and drive to Srinagar, pride of the beautiful valley of Kashmir famous for its lakes dotted with Shikaras and the Mughal Gardens. Check in the Hotel. Stay overnight at Houseboat in Srinagar.


Day 06 : Srinagar-Local sightseeing:After having the breakfast at the hotel, leave for Mughal Gardens visiting Nishat Bagh (Abode of pleasure) and Shalimar Bagh (Abode of love). All the gardens are situated on the bank of Dal Lake with the Zabarwan Mountains in the background. The distance from Srinagar to the said gardens is about 12 and 15 kms respectively. Evening back for Lake rides by Shikara to visit Floating Gardens, Lotus Gardens. Stay overnight at Houseboat in Srinagar


Day 07 : Srinagar-Gulmarg-Srinagar (112 Kms): After having the breakfast at the hotel, leave for Gulmarg (Meadow of Flowers) 2730 mts. above the sea level. The distance of 60 kms will be covered in about 2 hours. Gulmarg has one of the best ski slopes in the world and highest golf course with 18 holes. Also enjoy cable car (Gondola) for mountain ride upto khilangmarg. You can have a pony ride (By own Cost) in the open fields to Visits seven springs and to a Alpather lake, which remains frozen until June, Return back to Srinagar. Stay overnight at Hotel in Srinagar.


Day 08 : Srinagar-Sonmarg-Srinagar (168 Kms):After having the breakfast at the hotel, Full day excursion to Sonmarg (Meadows of Gold), Sonmarg lies in Sindh Valley streamed with flowers, surrounded by mountains and perched at an altitude of 2690 m above sea level. You may take one can have a pony ride to visit Thajiwas Glacier where snow remains round the year the major attraction at Sonamarg in summer months, Sonamarg is known as Gateway of Leh – Ladakh. Return back to Srinagar. Stay overnight at Hotel in Srinagar.


Day 09 : Srinagar-Pahalgam (186 Kms):After having the breakfast at the hotel, and drive to Pahalgam 2440 Mtrs (Vale of Kashmir) on the way visit Saffron fields and Avantipur ruins which is eleven hundred years old temple. Later walk along pony trails with a magnificent view of the mountains, and can go to Chandanwadi, village ardu and others (Sightseeing By Horse Ride), Finally by the lunch time you will reach Pahalgam which is the most famous place for Indian Film Industry. After lunch enjoy the nature charm of the valley. Stay overnight at Hotel in Pahalgam.


Day 10 : Pahalgam to Jammu: After having the breakfast at the hotel, drive to Jammu and drop at Railway Station to board the Train/ Airport for onward/return journey with sweet memories.

The temple houses three main images, which is Sakyamuni Buddha, Avalokitesvara and Padmasambhava, then visit Bhagsu Nag Temple and waterfall, charming church of St. John, Norbulingka Institute and then back to the Hotel, Stay overnight at Hotel in Dharamsala.


Day 06 : Dharamsala to Manali (275kms): After having the Breakfast at the Hotel, proceed to Manali, Manali is a perfect hill station covered with Snow Mountains and beautiful waterfalls. On arrival check in at hotel, spend rest of the day at leisure, Stay overnight at Hotel in Manali


Day 07 : Rohtangpas :After having the Breakfast at the Hotel, Go for full day visit to Rohtang Pass -(tourist spot in himalaya having snow all 12 months) is the most visited tourist spot in Himalaya having snow round the year (Upto Snow Line in case Rohtang is closed due to snow fall) and Sollang Valley evening return to hotel. Stay overnight at Hotel in Manali.


Day 08 : Hadimba Mata:After having the Breakfast at the Hotel, visit to Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple, Tibetan Monastery, Vasishtha Bath & Club House, In evening free for shopping activities, Stay overnight at Hotel in Manali.


Day 09 : Manali to Shimla (275kms) :After having the Breakfast at the Hotel, drive to Shimla, a beautiful hill station, set amidst the snow capped Shivalik mountain range offering some stunning views of the mighty Himalayas. Upon arrival check in the hotel, spend rest of the day at leisure, Stay overnight at Hotel in Shimla.


Day 10 : Shimla: After having the Breakfast at the Hotel, Full day excursion tour to Kufri, Kufri situated at an altitude of 2500 meters just an hours drive from Shimla and a very popular skiing resort during the winter months. Later visit Naldehra, Kamna Devi Temple, Lakkar Bazar, Church etc, Evening free to stroll in the famous shopping place of Shimla town – The Mall & The Ridge, Stay overnight at Hotel in Shimla.


Day 11 : Shimla to Chandigarh (120kms): After having the Breakfast at the Hotel, proceed to Chandigarh by road. Chandigarh is first planned city of modern India, Check in the Hotel, in evening visit to Rock Garden, Rose Garden, Sukhna Lake, Sector 17 Market for shopping, Stay overnight at Hotel in Chandigarh.


Day 12 : Departure: After having the Breakfast at the Hotel, drop at Chandigarh Railway Station to board the Train/ Airport for onward/return journey.
